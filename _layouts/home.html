Welcome to the Zhao Food Science Lab!

The Zhao Food Science Lab at San Diego State University studies protein chemistry, flavor chemistry, alternative proteins, and data-driven tools for food science. Our research explores how food proteins and flavor compounds influence functionality, sensory quality, stability, and overall food performance.

Current research areas include functional characterization of novel food proteins, flavor generation from alternative protein sources, bitterness prediction in microalgae, molecular dynamics simulation of food proteins, and machine learning, deep learning, and AI-based modeling for protein functionality and food quality prediction.

By combining laboratory experiments, molecular-level analysis, curated datasets, and computational modeling, our lab aims to support the development of sustainable, nutritious, and high-quality protein-based foods.
