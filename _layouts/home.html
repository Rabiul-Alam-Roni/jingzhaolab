Welcome to the Zhao Food Science Lab!

The Zhao Food Science Lab at San Diego State University studies food protein functionality, flavor chemistry, and alternative proteins. Our work focuses on improving the value, quality, and application of protein-rich foods from plant, algae, insect, and agricultural by-product sources.

We are interested in how proteins contribute to texture, flavor, stability, and overall food quality. Current research areas include rice bran protein utilization, plant and algae proteins for alternative foods, C-phycocyanin stabilization from spirulina, and the development of meat and seafood flavors from alternative proteins.
