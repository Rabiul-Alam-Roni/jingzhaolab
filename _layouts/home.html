Welcome to the Zhao Food Science Lab!

The Zhao Food Science Lab at San Diego State University studies protein chemistry, flavor chemistry, alternative proteins, and data-driven tools for food science. Our research focuses on understanding how food proteins and flavor compounds influence functionality, sensory quality, stability, and overall food performance.

Current research areas include the functional characterization of novel proteins from plant, algae, insect, dairy, marine, and agricultural by-product sources; flavor generation and flavor chemistry of alternative proteins; bitterness prediction in microalgae; molecular dynamics simulation of food proteins; and machine learning, deep learning, and AI-based modeling for protein functionality and food quality prediction.

By combining laboratory experiments, molecular-level analysis, literature-based datasets, and computational modeling, our lab aims to advance sustainable, nutritious, and high-quality protein-based foods.
